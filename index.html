Our Story
"We exist to make orbital access as reliable as crossing an ocean — precise, repeatable, and available to all."

Founded by a team of aerospace veterans and entrepreneurs, OrbitX has redefined what commercial launch can look like. We combine military-grade engineering with the agility of a technology company, delivering end-to-end satellite deployment services with an unmatched success record.

Our four-vehicle fleet covers every orbital regime from low-Earth to lunar transfer, with per-mission customisation that no legacy provider can match. We don't just launch satellites — we architect missions.

Milestones
Oct 1969
Institutional Genesis
Established with an initial capital allocation of $180M, laying the groundwork for indigenous launch systems and orbital engineering.
Jul 1980
First Successful Orbital Injection
Executed first successful orbital deployment, validating early-stage launch vehicle architecture with sub-ton payload capability.
Sep 1994
PSLV Operationalisation
PSLV inducted — enabling cost-efficient multi-orbit deployments at approximately $6K/kg, redefining commercial launch economics.
Apr 2001
GSLV Induction
GSLV introduced, expanding heavy-lift capability with missions exceeding $50M per launch into geostationary transfer trajectories.
Aug 2014
Cryogenic Propulsion Breakthrough
Successful flight validation of indigenously developed cryogenic upper stage, marking a pivotal advancement in high-efficiency propulsion systems.
Jan 2025
247 Mission Milestone
Surpassed 247 successful missions, generating cumulative launch value exceeding $12B, across ASLV, PSLV, and GSLV systems.
